# Lekha - Card Game Rules

## Overview
Lekha is a 4-player trick-taking card game played with Uno-styled cards. Partners sit opposite each other. The goal is to avoid points - the first team to reach 101 points loses.

## Card Mapping (Uno to Traditional)

### Suits
| Traditional Suit | Uno Color |
|------------------|-----------|
| Hearts ♥ | Red |
| Diamonds ♦ | Yellow |
| Spades ♠ | Blue |
| Clubs ♣ | Green |

### Special Cards
| Uno Card | Traditional | Points |
|----------|-------------|--------|
| Yellow 0 | 10 of Diamonds | **10 points** |
| Blue +2 | Queen of Spades | **13 points** |
| Blue Reverse | Jack of Spades | 0 points |
| Blue Block/Skip | King of Spades | 0 points |
| Any 1 | Ace | 0 points |
| All Red cards | Hearts | **1 point each** |

### Card Rankings (High to Low)
Within each suit: K (Block), Q (+2), J (Reverse), 10 (0), 9, 8, 7, 6, 5, 4, 3, 2, A (1)

**Note**: For Blue suit, +2 represents Queen, so it ranks between King (Block) and Jack (Reverse).

## Players
- **4 players** exactly
- **2 teams** of 2 (partners sit across from each other)
- Player positions: North, East, South, West
- Teams: North+South vs East+West

## Setup
1. Shuffle all 52 cards
2. Deal 13 cards to each player
3. Players view their hands

## Pass Phase (Before Each Round)
1. Each player selects 3 cards from their hand
2. All players simultaneously pass their 3 cards to the player on their **right**
3. Players add received cards to their hand

## Gameplay (Trick-Taking)

### Starting a Round
- First round: Predetermined starting player
- After someone takes the Blue +2 (Queen of Spades): Next round starts from the player to their **right**

### Playing a Trick
1. **Lead player** plays any card from their hand
2. Other players play **one card each**, clockwise
3. **Must follow suit**: If you have cards of the led suit, you must play one of them
4. **Can't follow**: If you have no cards of the led suit, play any card

### Winning a Trick
- The **highest card of the led suit** wins the trick
- Cards of other suits cannot win (no trumps)
- Winner collects all 4 cards and leads the next trick

### Card Ranking (within suit)
Highest to Lowest: K, Q, J, 10, 9, 8, 7, 6, 5, 4, 3, 2, A

## Scoring

### Point Cards
| Card | Points |
|------|--------|
| Any Red card (Hearts) | 1 point |
| Yellow 0 (10 of Diamonds) | 10 points |
| Blue +2 (Queen of Spades) | 13 points |

### Maximum Points Per Round
- 13 Hearts = 13 points
- Yellow 0 = 10 points
- Blue +2 = 13 points
- **Total: 36 points per round**

### Team Scoring
- Both partners' points are combined
- After each round, add new points to team totals

## Winning and Losing
- **Game ends** when any team reaches **101 points or more**
- **That team LOSES** (both partners lose together)
- The other team wins

## Special Rules

### Blue +2 (Queen of Spades) Rule
When a player takes the Blue +2 in a trick:
- They receive the 13 points
- **Next round** begins with the player to their **right** leading first

## Strategy Tips
1. **Avoid Hearts**: Each one costs 1 point
2. **Avoid Yellow 0**: Costs 10 points
3. **Avoid Blue +2**: Costs 13 points - most dangerous card
4. **Pass wisely**: Get rid of high cards in weak suits
5. **Protect your partner**: Sometimes take points to save them from more
6. **Count cards**: Track what's been played

## Terminology
- **Trick**: One round where each player plays one card
- **Lead**: Playing the first card of a trick
- **Follow suit**: Playing a card of the same suit as the lead
- **Break hearts**: First time a heart is played (led or discarded)
